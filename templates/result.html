{% elif category == "Normal weight" %}

Health Implications: Generally considered healthy; lower risk of weight-related diseases.

Tips to Maintain Health:

  • Diet: Maintain a balanced diet with fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and healthy fats.
  • Exercise: Regular physical activity such as 150 minutes of moderate exercise per week.
  • Lifestyle: Stay hydrated, manage stress, and monitor weight periodically.
{% elif category == "Overweight" %}

Health Implications: Increased risk of cardiovascular diseases, diabetes, and joint issues.

Tips to Improve Health:

  • Diet: Adopt portion control, reduce sugary and processed foods, and increase fiber intake.
  • Exercise: Engage in regular cardio (like walking, running, or cycling) and strength training.
  • Lifestyle: Develop a consistent meal plan, avoid late-night snacking, and monitor progress.
{% elif category == "Obesity" %}

Health Implications: Higher risk of heart disease, diabetes, sleep apnea, and certain cancers.

Tips to Improve Health:

  • Diet: Work with a dietitian to create a calorie-controlled meal plan focused on whole foods.
  • Exercise: Start with low-impact activities like swimming or walking, gradually increasing intensity.
  • Lifestyle: Set realistic weight-loss goals, seek support from health professionals, and address emotional eating.
